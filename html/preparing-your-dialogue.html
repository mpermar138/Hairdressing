At the hairdresser's

Preparing your dialogue

Task

This is the task you will have to perform.

Prepare a dialogue with a classmate. Then, you will have to perform it in front of the class.


Here's an example:

Hairdresser: Hi! Welcome. What would you like to have done today?

Client: Hi, thanks. I’m thinking about getting some highlights.

Hairdresser: Great choice! Do you have a specific color in mind, or would you like me to recommend something that suits your tone?

Client: I’m open to suggestions. Also… I’m worried about hair loss. Could you recommend something to help prevent it?

Hairdresser: Of course. For highlights, I’d suggest a warm caramel tone—it will brighten your hair without too much damage.
As for preventing hair fall, you can start with a strengthening shampoo and conditioner, preferably sulfate-free. I also recommend using a nourishing mask once a week.

Client: That sounds good. Anything else I should do?

Hairdresser: Try not to over-wash your hair, avoid very tight hairstyles, and apply a heat-protectant spray if you use a blow-dryer or a straightener. That helps reduce breakage.

Client: Perfect, thank you! Let’s go with the caramel highlights then.

Hairdresser: Wonderful! Let’s get started.
